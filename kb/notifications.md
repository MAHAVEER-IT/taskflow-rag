# Notifications

## Overview

TaskFlow notifications help you stay informed about tasks, comments, mentions, due dates, project updates, invitations, and billing events. Notifications can appear in email, inside TaskFlow, or both.

Each user controls their personal notification preferences. Admins can configure some workspace-level notification defaults, but individual users can decide which updates they want to receive.

## Steps

1. Sign in to TaskFlow.
2. Select your profile image or initials.
3. Open **Notification preferences**.
4. Choose the notification channels you want to use: **Email** and **In-app**.
5. Turn categories on or off, such as task assignments, mentions, due dates, project changes, and file comments.
6. Set a notification frequency, such as immediate, daily summary, or weekly summary.
7. Choose quiet hours if you do not want non-urgent notifications during certain times.
8. Select **Save preferences**.

To view in-app notifications, select the bell icon in the top navigation. Unread notifications are marked until opened. You can mark all notifications as read from the notification panel.

To disable most notifications, turn off the categories you no longer want. Security, billing, and account verification emails may still be sent because they are required for account management.

## Important Notes

- Email notifications are sent to the verified email address on your TaskFlow account.
- In-app notifications are visible only when you are signed in.
- Mentions using `@name` usually override summary settings and may be sent immediately.
- You cannot disable required security emails, password reset messages, or billing failure alerts.
- Project-specific notification settings may override general workspace preferences.
- Notification changes may take a few minutes to apply.

## Troubleshooting

If you are not receiving email notifications, check your spam, junk, promotions, or quarantine folders. Confirm that your email address is verified in **Profile settings > Account**.

If you receive too many notifications, switch task and project updates to a daily summary or disable categories that are not relevant to your work.

If in-app notifications are missing, refresh TaskFlow and confirm that you still have access to the project or task.

If a teammate does not receive mentions, confirm that you used the correct name and that the person has access to the project.

## FAQ

**Can I turn off all emails?**  
You can disable most email categories, but required account, security, and billing emails may still be sent.

**Can I receive only daily summaries?**  
Yes. Set your frequency to **Daily summary** in notification preferences.

**Do notification settings apply to all workspaces?**  
Personal settings apply to your account, but some project and workspace settings may vary by workspace.

**Why did I get notified about a task I do not own?**  
You may have been mentioned, added as a watcher, or assigned to a related task.
