# Account Creation

## Overview

TaskFlow accounts give you access to workspaces, projects, tasks, files, notifications, and integrations. A workspace is the shared area where your team manages projects together. Most new users create an account, verify their email address, and then create or join their first workspace.

Use the same email address that your team expects to invite. If you receive an invitation before signing up, create your account with the invited email address so TaskFlow can connect the invitation automatically.

## Steps

1. Go to the TaskFlow sign-up page.
2. Enter your full name, work email address, and a secure password.
3. Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
4. Select **Create account**.
5. Open the verification email from TaskFlow and select **Verify email**.
6. Return to TaskFlow and sign in if prompted.
7. Create your first workspace by entering a workspace name, such as your company or department name.
8. Choose your workspace region and default time zone.
9. Select **Create workspace**.
10. Add team members now, or skip this step and invite them later from **Workspace settings > Members**.

After your workspace is created, TaskFlow opens the project dashboard. From there, you can create a project, invite team members, upload files, and configure notifications.

## Important Notes

- You must verify your email address before you can create projects, invite team members, or manage billing.
- Passwords should be unique to TaskFlow and should not be reused from other services.
- Workspace names can be changed later by an Admin in **Workspace settings**.
- If your company uses single sign-on, you may need to select **Continue with SSO** instead of creating a password.
- Invitations expire after 14 days. Ask an Admin or Manager to resend the invitation if the link has expired.

## Troubleshooting

If you do not receive the verification email, check your spam, junk, promotions, or quarantine folder. Search for emails from `notifications@taskflow.example`. You can also select **Resend verification email** from the sign-in screen.

If TaskFlow says your email is already registered, use **Forgot password** instead of creating a second account. If you signed up with the wrong email address, create a new account with the correct address or ask Support to help merge access.

If the sign-up page does not load, refresh the browser, clear cached site data, or try a current version of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari.

## FAQ

**Can I create more than one workspace?**  
Yes. After signing in, open the workspace switcher and select **Create new workspace**.

**Can I join a workspace after creating my account?**  
Yes. Ask an Admin or Manager to invite the email address connected to your TaskFlow account.

**Why do I need email verification?**  
Email verification protects your account and confirms that project updates, password resets, and invitations reach the correct inbox.

**Can I change my email address later?**  
Yes. Go to **Profile settings > Account** and update your email address. You must verify the new address before it becomes active.
