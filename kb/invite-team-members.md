# Invite Team Members

## Overview

TaskFlow lets Admins and Managers invite people to a workspace so they can collaborate on projects, tasks, comments, and uploaded files. Invitations are sent by email and include a secure link to join the workspace.

New users can create a TaskFlow account during the invitation flow. Existing users can accept the invitation after signing in with the invited email address.

## Steps

1. Open your TaskFlow workspace.
2. Select **Workspace settings** from the sidebar.
3. Select **Members**.
4. Choose **Invite members**.
5. Enter one or more email addresses.
6. Select a default role: **Admin**, **Manager**, or **Member**.
7. Add an optional message explaining which projects the invitee will work on.
8. Select **Send invitations**.

To accept an invitation, the recipient should open the email from TaskFlow, select **Accept invitation**, and sign in or create an account using the invited email address. After accepting, they appear in the workspace member list.

To resend an invitation, go to **Workspace settings > Members > Pending invitations**, select the invitation, and choose **Resend**.

To remove a user, open **Members**, select the user’s menu, and choose **Remove from workspace**. Confirm the removal when prompted.

## Important Notes

- Only Admins can invite users as Admins.
- Managers can usually invite Members unless an Admin has restricted this permission.
- Pending invitations expire after 14 days.
- Removing a user does not delete tasks, comments, or files they created.
- A removed user immediately loses access to workspace projects, private files, and in-app notifications.
- If your subscription has a seat limit, you may need to upgrade before inviting more users.

## Troubleshooting

If a team member cannot find the invitation, ask them to check spam, junk, promotions, or quarantine folders. You can also resend the invitation from **Pending invitations**.

If the invitation opens the wrong workspace, confirm that the recipient signed in with the same email address listed on the invitation. Email aliases can prevent TaskFlow from matching the invite correctly.

If you cannot see the invite option, your role may not have permission to invite users. Ask an Admin to update your role or send the invitation.

## FAQ

**Can I invite multiple people at once?**  
Yes. Enter multiple email addresses separated by commas or line breaks.

**Can I change a user’s role after inviting them?**  
Yes. Admins can update roles from **Workspace settings > Members**.

**Can guests be invited to only one project?**  
TaskFlow uses workspace roles and project visibility. For limited access, add the person as a Member and invite them only to specific private projects.

**What happens to pending invitations when I downgrade?**  
If your new plan has fewer seats, some pending invitations may be paused until seats are available.
