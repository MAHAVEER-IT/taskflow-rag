# Billing and Subscriptions

## Overview

TaskFlow subscriptions determine which features, seat limits, storage limits, integrations, and support options are available to your workspace. Admins can manage plans, payment methods, invoices, and billing contacts from workspace billing settings.

Billing applies at the workspace level. If you belong to multiple workspaces, each workspace may have its own subscription and payment method.

## Steps

1. Open the workspace you want to manage.
2. Select **Workspace settings**.
3. Select **Billing**.
4. Review your current plan, renewal date, seat usage, and storage usage.
5. To upgrade, select **Change plan**, choose a higher plan, review the price, and confirm.
6. To downgrade, select **Change plan**, choose a lower plan, review feature changes, and confirm.
7. To update payment details, select **Payment methods**.
8. Add a new card or update the existing billing method.
9. To view invoices, select **Invoices** and download the needed receipt.
10. Save any billing contact changes before leaving the page.

Plan changes may take effect immediately or at the next renewal date depending on the type of change.

## Important Notes

- Only Admins can manage billing and subscriptions.
- Upgrades usually take effect immediately and may be prorated for the current billing period.
- Downgrades usually take effect at the next renewal date.
- If your downgrade exceeds project, seat, storage, or integration limits, TaskFlow will show what must be changed first.
- Failed payments may place the workspace into a limited-access state until payment is resolved.
- Refunds are reviewed according to the TaskFlow refund policy and may depend on billing date, plan type, and account status.

## Troubleshooting

If a payment fails, confirm the card number, expiration date, billing address, and available funds. You may also need to approve the transaction with your bank.

If you cannot downgrade, review the warnings shown on the billing page. You may need to remove seats, archive projects, reduce storage, or disconnect premium integrations.

If an invoice is missing, check that you are viewing the correct workspace. Billing records are not shared across workspaces.

If your workspace is locked for payment, update the payment method and select **Retry payment**.

## FAQ

**Can Managers access billing?**  
No. Billing is available only to Admins.

**Will I lose data if I downgrade?**  
TaskFlow does not delete your data automatically, but access to features above your new plan limits may be restricted.

**Can I change from monthly to annual billing?**  
Yes. Open **Billing > Change plan** and select the annual billing option.

**How do I request a refund?**  
Contact TaskFlow Support from **Help > Contact support** and include your workspace name, invoice number, and reason for the request.
